About MISC

Messina International Students Cooperative (MISC) was founded to bridge the gap between international students and the essential services they need to thrive in Italy. We are a student-led community that helps international students feel at home in Messina. Together, we work for our development, unlock our potential, and support each other to thrive academically, socially, and personally.

Who We Are

A dedicated team of fellow students and locals committed to your success in Messina.

🔭

Our Vision

To be the leading support system for international students in Messina, creating a seamless bridge between academic aspirations and practical life needs, fostering a thriving global student community.

🎯

Our Mission

To provide comprehensive, reliable, and accessible services that empower international students to focus on their studies while we handle the complexities of living in a new country.
